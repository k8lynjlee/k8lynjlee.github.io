Electrical Engineering Internships
Hewlett-Packard
Summer 2011

During my internship at Hewlett-Packard I learned Python on my own and assisted another intern with writing a program to collect, parse, sort, and search printer XML data. I also tested different versions of firmware with printer prototypes for required features. My supervisors commended me for my positive attitude, flexibility, ability to learn quickly and communicate with others, and meaningful contributions to the team.

Bonneville Power Administration
Summer 2010

During my internship at the Bonneville Power Administration I designed and built 25 milliamp DC supply transducer simulator devices, which output a range of current to test power transmission equipment in the field. These devices are now being used in each district that BPA serves, such as during installation or maintenance, and also in training programs for BPA engineers. I also analyzed high-speed voltage lab videos of disconnect switches to find the start/stop arc and commutation times.
